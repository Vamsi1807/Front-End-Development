Cricket

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game between two teams of 11 players each. One team tries to score runs by hitting the ball and running between wickets, while the other team tries to dismiss them and prevent runs. It's like baseball, but with a round bat and wickets instead of bases. There are different formats, with some matches lasting hours and others just a few!

Formats in cricket :

Format No of overs Additional source
Test 90 overs a day for 5 days Test cricket
ODI 50 overs ODI cricket
T20 20 overs T20 cricket
